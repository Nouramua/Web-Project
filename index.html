Hayyhum

Explore Saudi, One Experience at a Time

Vision

To become the leading digital gateway for exploring Saudi Arabia’s diverse cities, heritage, and experiences. Empowering travelers to discover authentic journeys while supporting local tourism and hospitality.

Mission

Hayyhum connects visitors with local service providers across Saudi Arabia, offering a simple front-end interface to explore,  request, and review tourism services. The platform promotes cultural heritage and encourages sustainable local tourism.

Goals

Hayyhum mainly aim to promote the tourism in Saudi by giving an opportunity to explore it's different regions to reflect the cultural diversity encouraging tourism.

Services

Experiences The Saudi Way

From desert adventures to cultural tours, discover tailored experiences that let you explore Saudi Arabia at your own pace.
